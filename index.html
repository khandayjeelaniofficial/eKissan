e-Kissan Overview

Electronic Trading Platform (e-Kissan) is an online electronic B2B like trading portal for all farmers. It is basically made to integrate markets first at the region level and in future across districts, States, and the country through a common market platform, to facilitate trade in agricultural commodities, especially fruits.

Our Vision

To integrate markets at the region level through a common online market platform, to facilitate trade in agricultural commodities especially fruits.

To promote better marketing opportunities for farmers / sellers through online access to more buyers / markets.
